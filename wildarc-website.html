The Land Chapter

10 acres carved from the mist-soaked hills of Kodagu

Chettimani is WildArc's living laboratory — a food forest and regenerative farm where silver oaks stretch through morning fog, cardamom scents the air, and every planted tree becomes a data point in an open ecological record.

We're restoring native biodiversity while building sustainable livelihoods. The coffee we trade, the trees we plant, the ponds we dig — all of it feeds back into a platform that anyone can use, anywhere in the world.

The Long View

"We are building a living record of what it takes to heal a landscape — not for profit, but for the long memory of the land itself."

WildArc is a 10-year project in three acts: first, we plant and document. Then, we teach and open the gates. Finally, we replicate — seeding regenerative practices in forests and farms across India and beyond.

Act I · Now
Plant & Document
Food forest, infrastructure, the living database. Proof that it works, in the soil of Coorg.
Act II · 2027+
Teach & Open
Experiences, programs, OSS launch. The platform matures and spreads to other hands.
Act III · 2030+
Replicate & Scale
New sites, new stewards, new landscapes. One forest at a time.
