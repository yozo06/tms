# 🌿 WildArc — The Living Ecosystem Platform

> *A data-driven, open-source model for regenerative permaculture — born in Coorg, built for the world.*

---

## What Is WildArc?

WildArc is a **modular, open-source software ecosystem** designed to digitize, track, and optimize **regenerative permaculture farms**. Rather than a single monolithic app, WildArc mirrors the very ecosystems it manages — built as **interconnected modules**, each representing a natural layer of a self-sustaining food forest.

Every data point collected — every tree tagged, every health check logged, every yield recorded — contributes to a **living intelligence**. By proving what works on the ground with real data, WildArc aims to become an **open teaching model** that empowers farmers and communities worldwide to replicate self-sustaining ecosystems.

## The Ecosystem Suite

| Module | Layer | Status | Description |
|--------|-------|--------|-------------|
| **🌳 Arbor** | Canopy | ✅ Active | Trees, large perennials, and spatial tracking |
| **🌿 Flora** | Understory | 🔜 Next | Companion plants, vines, crops, and symbiotic Guilds |
| **🪨 Terra** | Earth | 📋 Planned | Soil telemetry, topography, water flow, and land health |
| **🍄 Myco** | Underground | 💡 Future | Fungi, soil microbiology, and holistic balance tracking |
| **🧠 Synapse** | Intelligence | 💡 Future | Cross-module Mind Maps, Guild analytics, and AI insights |
